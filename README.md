# pandemic-modeling

These files are all in netlogo's language, and they can be run on netlogo software or on the web. (though I would recommend downloading the software)

degrees of distance.nlogo allows you to model the spread of a disease through a population by adjusting a number of diffeerent variables. Unique to this model (when compared to other Covid-19 models), is that we have broken the act of social distancing into two different dimensions:
  1. how many people are social distancing
  2. how aggressively they are social distancing
